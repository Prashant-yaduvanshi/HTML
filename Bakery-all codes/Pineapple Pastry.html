Pineapple Pastry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour.
  • 1 tsp of baking powder.
  • 3 eggs.
  • 1/2 cup sugar.
  • 1/2 cup Pineapple juice.
  • 1/2 cup oil.
  • Pineapple.

Notes on Ingredients

Pineapple
Use crushed pineapple and it should be drained.

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 385 degree F(200 degrees C).
  2. Grease and line an 8" round tin.
  3. Sieve the flours and the baking powder together 3 times.
  4. Using an electric blender beat the egg whites to form soft peaks.
  5. Gradually add sugar and beat till thick.

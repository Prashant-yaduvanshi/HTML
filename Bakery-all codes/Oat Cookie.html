Oat Cookie

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter.
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar.
  • 1 banana.
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats.
  • 3/4 cup maida.
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda.
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder.
  • 1/4 tsp salt.
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract.

NOtes on Ingredients

Sugar
Sugar should be used in powdered form.

Method

  1. In a large mixing bowl take butter.
  2. Add brown sugar and beat well.
  3. Further,add banana and beat again.
  4. Now add rolled oats and maida.
  5. Also add baking soda,cinnamon powder, salt and vanilla extract.

Black Forest Pastry

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup plain flour.
  • 3 table spoon coca powder.
  • 1 cup sugar.
  • 1 tsp baking powder.
  • 1 pinch baking soda.
  • 3 eggs.
  • 3/4 cup oil.
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup fesh cream.
  • A pinch salt.

Notes on Ingredients

Sugar
Sugar should be used in powdered form.

Method

  1. Sieve flour, cocoa, baking powder,baking soda and salt together.
  2. In a large bowl beat oil and powdered sugar until pale.
  3. Add 1 egg at a time and beat well.
  4. Add fresh cream and beat.
  5. Add in flour cocoa mixture and fold thoroughly.

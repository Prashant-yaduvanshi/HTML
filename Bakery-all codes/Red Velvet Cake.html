Red Velvet Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 2/3 cups cake flour
  • 2 tsp coca flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter softened
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs room temperature
  • 2 1/2 tsp red food coloring
  • 1 cup buttermilk room temperature
  • 2 tsp white vinegar

Note on Ingredients

Flour
if you don't have cake flour you can use a combination of cornstarch & all-purpose flour.

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F degrees.
  2. Grease and lightly flour a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  3. Sift together the flour, coca, bakin soda and salt.whisk gently to cobine.
  4. In a sepatate large bown beat together the butter ad sugar until fluffy.
  5. Beat in the oil, followed by the eggs,vanilla extract & red food coloring.
  6. Beat in the flour mixture into the butter mixture about 1/3 at a time, alternating with about 1/3 of the buttermilk. Turn off the mixer and scrape down the sides of the bow as needed.
  7. Beat in the vinegar.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean.
